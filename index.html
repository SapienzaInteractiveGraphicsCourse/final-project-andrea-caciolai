Link's Training

Instructions

Theme

Help Link master his archery skill. His arrows are magical, so each time he hits the target, a new arrow respawns in his quiver. However, each time he misses the target, the arrow does not respawn, so you have at most three chances to miss, after which it is game over! You cannot go too close to the target, so position yourself in a good spot, aim, and shoot. Remember, gravity matters!

Controls

Move Link: WASD
Move camera: MOUSE
Aim: Mouse2
Shoot: Mouse1

Difficulty

Easy: Target is still
Medium: Target moves horizontally
Hard: Target moves along a 8-shaped path.
